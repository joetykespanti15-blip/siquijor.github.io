If you choose to visit the mystical island of Siquijor, we promise you an enchanting escape where the warmth of local hospitality meets the breathtaking beauty of turquoise waterfalls and world-class violet sunsets. You will leave with a heart full of wonder and a spirit refreshed by the island’s unique blend of ancient folklore, pristine white-sand beaches, and serene coastal charm.

HOTELS

Enjoying the hotels in Siquijor is a truly immersive experience, offering everything from luxurious beachfront villas at Coco Grove that overlook marine sanctuaries to unique, eco-friendly jungle treehouses and cliffside resorts where you can wake up to panoramic ocean views.

TOURIST ATTRACTIONS

Exploring the tourist attractions in Siquijor is a journey through a land of wonders, where you can swing like Tarzan into the turquoise pools of Cambugahay Falls, receive a natural fish spa at the 400-year-old Enchanted Balete Tree, and marvel at the Spanish-era grandeur of the Lazi Church and Convent.
